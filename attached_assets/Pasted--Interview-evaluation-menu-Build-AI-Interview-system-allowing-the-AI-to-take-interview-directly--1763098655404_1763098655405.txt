
Interview evaluation menu. 
Build AI Interview system allowing the AI to take interview directly for the Candidate. Once the Candidate login and start the interview, there will be few pleseantalries. Then the AI will start the interview process. AI will check the Job description created for the job, compare it with the Resume and decide the flow of the interview. Question be configured based on hte Candidate Experince (Fresher, Junior, Senior or Expert), the job title and department.  AI will generate questions based on Generate AI and Companies Question bank. Each company will have their own Question bank. The Interview will text based and the Candiate will answere back in text. An Interview Transction table has to be defied and details like, date and time, candidates name, phone, interview details at the question level, the response, AI review for each question, Summary of total questions asked, correct answere, overall rank out of 10 will be stored for future access. During the interview Video will be switched on and capture candiates movements for fraud etc. These video clips will also be stored for future reference. The link of th e video will be stored in the interview transaction table.

Architectural Flow:
Student → Video/Audio Stream → WebRTC/WebSocket 
       → Speech-to-Text → LangGraph AI Agent 
       → Dynamic Question Generation → Text-to-Speech

✅ Completed (Tasks 1-3):
1. Database Schema

interview_sessions: Tracks each interview session with exam type, subject, status, duration, and scores
interview_messages: Stores conversation history between AI and student
interview_evaluations: Stores detailed evaluation results with performance metrics
2. WebSocket Server
Real-time bidirectional communication channel at /ws/interview
Ready for live audio/video streaming and AI responses
3. Backend API Routes
All routes are protected with authentication:

POST /api/interviews - Create new interview session
GET /api/interviews - Get user's interview history
GET /api/interviews/:id - Get specific session details
PATCH /api/interviews/:id - Update session status
GET /api/interviews/:id/messages - Get interview conversation
GET /api/interviews/:id/evaluation - Get results and scores
