Product Requirement Document: AI Interview SaaS Platform
1. Product Overview
A cloud-based SaaS platform to automate and optimize candidate screening and interviews using AI. This system enables recruiters to post jobs, collect and rank applications, interview candidates using AI voice/text bots, and manage the entire hiring workflow through a unified dashboard.
 
2. User Roles
•	Company Admin: Manages platform, users, settings, and reporting.
•	Recruiter: Creates jobs, manages postings, screens and interviews candidates.
•	Candidate: Applies for jobs, participates in AI interviews.
 
3. Functional Requirements & Workflow
Step 1: Company Admin / Recruiter Login & Job Creation
•	Secure login (multi-factor authentication)
•	Create/edit job requirements: title, description, location, domain, mandatory/optional skills, experience, salary, interview mode (voice/text/video)
•	Save and manage job templates
Step 2: Job Posting & Distribution
•	Publish job to site job listing board
•	Option to post to selected external job boards via API/integration (e.g. LinkedIn, Naukri)
•	Manage active/inactive jobs
Step 3: Resume Collection
•	Candidate portal for job application uploads (PDF/DOC/JSON)
•	Integration with external job sites to fetch and sync resumes
•	Auto-collection of candidate contact info
Step 4: Resume Screening
•	AI-based resume parser extracts skills, experience, education and other attributes
•	Automated matching of resume attributes against job requirements
Step 5: Resume Ranking
•	AI algorithm ranks resumes by fit/match score
•	Display ranked candidate list with configurable filters/sorting
Step 6: Candidate Contact & Interview Scheduling
•	In-app/email/SMS notification to shortlisted candidates
•	Calendar integration for scheduling AI interview slots
•	Option for manual override (set interviewers, timings)
Step 7: AI Interviews
•	Automated chatbot conducts structured interviews (voice or text)
•	Video recording interface for voice interviews (browser-based, anti-cheat features)
•	Store and secure interview evidence (audio/text/video files)
•	Interview transcripts generated and stored
Step 8: Selection & Shortlist
•	Recruiter/company admin selects top X candidates (customizable per job/role)
•	Option to compare interview transcripts, scores, and resume side-by-side
Step 9: Summary & Detail Report Generation
•	Automated summary report for each candidate: match score, interview transcript, AI insights (sentiment, keyword analysis, behavioral signals)
•	Downloadable detailed reports (PDF, CSV) for panel reviews
Step 10: Email Results to Candidates
•	Automated result notifications for all candidates
•	Customized messaging templates for successful/rejected/status update outcomes
 
4. Dashboard & Admin Features
•	Central dashboard for all active jobs, candidate status, analytics, and recent activities
•	Filters for job status, candidate stage, date range, recruiter, and job posting channels
•	Performance analytics: time to fill, candidate drop-off, interview conversion rate
•	Access logs and role-based permissions management
 
5. Non-Functional Requirements
•	Responsive UI/UX (web/mobile tablets)
•	Secure data handling (GDPR, SOC2 compliance)
•	Robust audit logging and anti-cheat for interviews
•	API support for integrations with third-party job sites and calendar providers
•	Scalable cloud deployment (AWS, Azure, GCP)
•	Modular backend: microservices for resume parsing, AI scoring, video interview, notification
 
6. Assumptions & Constraints
•	Job board and calendar integrations depend on external API access and terms
•	AI models may require periodic fine-tuning for different industries
•	Video/audio recordings stored as encrypted files with retention policies
 
7. Reporting & Documentation
•	Automated system and candidate reports
•	User manual, onboarding guides, API documentation
 
8. Key Deliverables
•	Product specification & wireframes for each workflow step
•	UI mockups (Company Dashboard, Job Posting, Candidate List, AI Interview, Reports)
•	API documentation for job board/event integrations
•	Security and compliance checklist
